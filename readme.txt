CHEM TRIALS

Be a daredevil and fly your bi-plane over the hills and through towns. Spread the mysterious substance around the planet!
But beware, the government is on your tail with some nasty assault helicopters!

Controls:
W A S D - movement
Esc - credits

Created during Ludum Dare #38, a 48 hour game jam.
April 22nd - 24th
